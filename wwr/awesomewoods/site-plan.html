Overview

Purpose

What incredible the woodwork is, most when its art is at home. Sometimes we imagine how to decor the house, but it is complicated to find exactly what we want. In that situation, what should I do? buy something "good"? The solution is here, our company will make your ideas alive, and you could see your house as you dreamed it. Through the website, you could find ideas about how to decor, art treasures made in wood, and high quality of work. So come and visit us to know more.

Audience

The audience for my web page is adult people, it does not matter their ages because anyone can have the need to buy decor for their houses. The audience needs to have an idea of what the company does through a picture gallery and from the testimonies of our customers. They do not need to have much money to buy wood arts because the prices are accessible. As the customer can create their own artwork, there are no specific characteristics in our audience. The audience needs to have time to imagine and create the decor of their houses.

Content

Home page

Why do you need to visit this site? VISION - MISION “Awesome woods” is a company where ideas come to life. The company has modern tools to help the customers to create awesome artwork in wood. The only condition is the imagination, the rest is our job. Commonly, people want to make a difference, but they do not know-how. The secret is the decor, it is amazing how the decoration can convert a house into another one. With that in mind, the first thing to do is to come to this web page. The site will provide ideas, pictures, tips, testimonies, and a guide to start in the decoration world. Each content in this site has the purpose to expand the customers’ vision and to recognize until what point we can provide solutions to their requests. The visitors will come to this website because the information provided by it will be organized and friendly with any customer. It will be easy to understand and there will be shortcuts from each page. Additionally, the design will be striking for the great images of our artworks making the customer excited. Another reason the customers need to come to this site is the clear information the company will provide, explaining each process with text and videos (the videos will show testimonies and final designs). What does the site contain? CONTENT Also, the customers could find how to transform their ideas to reality with step-by-step guide included on the site, the place where the offices are, the social media profiles, the forms to get contact with the company, and the phone numbers to call the company for assistance.

Images for the Home page

REGISTER – CONTACT US

The website will have enough images or examples that the customers can take as a guide to create their own projects. Through templates posted on the site, the customers will be guided to implement their ideas and customize their designs according to their imagination and the space in their houses. Once they can complete their designs, the site will provide a way to send to us the information. It is necessary to register on the site to have access to the templates and design their decoration, additionally with the register the users would receive feedback, comments, news, offers, among others. The site will require general information to create an account like name, e-mail, decor preferences, and means of contact. The site will become the first way of communication between the customers to the company and vice versa. Everything related to the process of creation will be posted in the customers' accounts, so there will be updates from both sides.

PROCESS INFO - FEEDBACK

How does the company work? PROCESS INFO - FEEDBACK Once the design can be evaluated, the company will send a precise quote with the costs through the customer's account. It is important to highlight that the user's account on the website will have complete tools to create a design, modify requests, see notification from the company, request information, see quotes, accept or reject quotes, provide feedback (it would be a Tab Navigation). The customer could pay in an easy and quick manner with an option on the customer's page. The customers could add comments or feedback on the site. It should be on a separate page.
