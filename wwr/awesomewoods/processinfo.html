How does the company work? PROCESS INFO - FEEDBACK

Once the design can be evaluated, the company will send a precise quote with the costs through the customer's account. It is important to highlight that the user's account on the website will have complete tools to create a design, modify requests, see notification from the company, request information, see quotes, accept or reject quotes, provide feedback (it would be a Tab Navigation). The customer could pay in an easy and quick manner with an option on the customer's page. The customers could add comments or feedback on the site. It should be on a separate page.

Why do you need to visit this site? VISION - MISION “Awesome woods” is a company where ideas come to life. The company has modern tools to help the customers to create awesome artwork in wood. The only condition is the imagination, the rest is our job. Commonly, people want to make a difference, but they do not know-how. The secret is the decor, it is amazing how the decoration can convert a house into another one. With that in mind, the first thing to do is to come to this web page. The site will provide ideas, pictures, tips, testimonies, and a guide to start in the decoration world. Each content in this site has the purpose to expand the customers’ vision and to recognize until what point we can provide solutions to their requests.

The visitors will come to this website because the information provided by it will be organized and friendly with any customer. It will be easy to understand and there will be shortcuts from each page. Additionally, the design will be striking for the great images of our artworks making the customer excited. Another reason the customers need to come to this site is the clear information the company will provide, explaining each process with text and videos (the videos will show testimonies and final designs).
