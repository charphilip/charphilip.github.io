Overview

Purpose

Provide real evidences to our customers about the great adventure they can have if they decide to join us to the whitewater rafting trip. The site will show a first taste about the places we can visit, the activities they can enroll, and the amazing prices we offer. The site will try to attract customers through images, videos, testimonies about the rafting trips, making the clients feel curious to book a trip with us. Likewise, the customers will know all the information to communicate with our agents and to know all about our company. The main purpose is once they visit our page, they want to call us to start the best Whitewater rafting experience.

Audience

The people who will book with our company are young guys. They would be around 17 - 30 years old, it does not exclude older. Our audience commonly are students, couples who have married or singles. Generally, they want to break out their academic rutines participating in outdoor activities. They are able to organize groups of friends or relatives and plan a rafting trip, expecting to have a gratifying and unforgettable experience. Their plans are spontaneous, so they would access to our site through their phones as a first option, tablets, and PC's.
